Practical information

How to start?

  1. It is never too late. First of all, it's never too late to start riding a bike with panniers. I started cycling with panniers at the age of 44, but I know people who started cycling in their 50s and have already visited half of Europe. All you need is willingness and health.

  2. Equipment. Complete the appropriate equipment. Without it, there is no point in starting. Remember that you don't have to spend a lot of money to be able to travel by bike with panniers. I write about the selection of the main items of equipment in the Equipment chapter.

  3. Guides, forums. These are large bases of knowledge. Read on, get to know the opinion of different people. Make up your own mind.

  4. The first time is the worst. I remember my first time. Lots of unnecessary things. Poorly planned load in panniers. You will not learn this. This is what you learn over time. What you would not forget, remember that good nutrition is the most important during a bike trip. If you go outside the summer season, keep in mind that access to food may be limited in some regions of Poland. You can read about nutrition during the expedition Kitchen on the road

  5. Prepare yourself physically for the trip. I know from my own experience that the best way to prepare for such a trip is by walking 10-15 km on the hills at least 4 times a week.

  6. Mental effort. Such a trip is also a mental effort. If you are not a loner like me, maybe the first time you go on a trip with someone you know. I ride a bike alone and only alone. Once I was traveling with a friend for two days and I felt bad about it.

  7. Weather. Be sure to check the weather forecast for the region you are going to. I sometimes change my plans two days before departure. The rain hasn't hurt anyone yet. I drive in the rain a lot. if you are properly dressed it is not a problem. But if there are long-lasting downpours, storms and floods, it is better to choose another region. In 2020, I was going to the south of Poland. However, two days before departure, I changed the direction of the trip. And I was not mistaken. There were downpours and floods in the south of Poland. Then cycling is not fun. And such a trip must be just a pleasure.

  8. Train ride. If you go by train first, be aware that the train conductor may not always let you in with your bike. Despite, and you have bought a ticket. There is no such problem on local trains. But on Inter City trains it is already a lottery. Either he'll let you on the train or he won't.

  9. Wild sleepover. If you prefer to sleep in the wild in a tent, read my modest guide in Wild sleepover chapter. But get to know other people's opinions about it as well. There are many sites on the web about sleeping in the wild.
